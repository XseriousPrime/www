Gamer Head

This is Gamer Head, an app just for you! Let us jump in and get started!

This is an app made just for the gamer inside of you.

Have you ever been irritated by players who don't match your skill set? Well as a gamer myself, i find this to be a issue that ruins my gaming experience. That is why I made an app to fix it. Here's how:

Why? Research Method
I started the research with some personal brainstorming. I wanted to reach out to something that I experience everyday. I chose the gaming community because it is really close to my heart. I have held a controller in my hand from since I was in diapers playing Pajama Sam. I finally decided that I wanted to make an app that improved the gamer's lifestyle.
To understand what gamer's needed I went to the source. Luckly I had many sources avaliable to me through my own friends across the headset. I asked them multiple questions as, "what bothers you about gaming?" and with a follow up question like "how can we improve that issue?". With the information I had it was now time to pick a research method and go from there.
I chose the method "The Five Whys". I have previously used this method for a ethnographic research I did on my love for video games and it opened up a lot of undiscovered doors for me. The person I interviewed is my boyfriend Mahan. Looking at how we met over the mic I thought he would be the perfect canidate for my research. After the interview with Mahan I found out that he would want a app that would help us connect with gamers of a similar skill level and interest. This help me finalize the idea of The Project. The Project will be an app that connects people with similar skill level.
